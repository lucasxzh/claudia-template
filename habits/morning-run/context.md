# Habit Context: Morning Run

## Why
To break the cycle of morning lethargy and WFH inertia. Initiating movement immediately upon waking sets a positive momentum for the day.

## Rules of Engagement
- **Frequency:** Mon - Fri (Weekends off). **DO NOT LOG MISSES ON WEEKENDS.**
- **Minimum Viable Threshold:** 5 minutes of running.
  - Location: Treadmill or Outside.
  - Intensity: Any. Just run.

## The Trigger
- **Primary:** Set an alarm or environmental cue that forces physical movement out of bed.
- **Action:** Get up immediately, put on running gear.

## Backup Protocols
- **If** feeling ill or injured, **Then** stretch or walk for 5 minutes (any point during the day).
- **If** weather is bad, **Then** use a treadmill or indoor alternative.

## Active Experiments
- **Cold Exposure:** Optional cold shower/rinse after the run. Not required for "success" status, but tracked in notes.
